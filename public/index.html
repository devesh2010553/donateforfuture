The ₹5.77 Crore: Every Rupee Explained

Split into two inviolable pillars — no blending, no raiding operations for construction, or vice versa.

Pillar 1 — CAPEX

Initial Infrastructure

₹1.55 Crore
  • 3-Story Building on 2 Bighas

    Bare-minimum, robust RCC construction. No luxury finishes. Structural integrity over aesthetics.

  • 14 Classrooms (NC/KG → Class 12)

    One classroom per grade level. Standard furniture, blackboards, basic lighting.

  • Basic Science & Computer Labs

    Functional labs meeting UP Board minimum requirements for senior secondary.

  • UP Board Compliance Infrastructure

    Library, principal's office, staff rooms, toilets, boundary wall as per UP Board norms.

  • Water, Electricity & Sanitation

    Borewell, solar panels for reduced recurring costs, separate toilets for girls.

Pillar 2 — OPEX

10-Year Running Runway

₹4.22 Crore
  • 14 Teachers @ ₹8,000/month

    Enforced low-cost salary model. B.Ed. qualified, UP Board approved. Welfare mission over market rates.

  • Principal + 5 Support Staff

    Administrative, peon, cook, security, and cleaning — all at set welfare rates.

  • Daily Basic Nutrition Meals

    Mid-day meal program for every enrolled child. Dal, rice, sabzi — minimum nutrition, 10-year budget.

  • Book Bank (5% Inflation Adjusted)

    Textbooks, stationery, uniforms. Cost modeled with 5% annual inflation for all 10 years.

  • Utility & Maintenance Reserve

    Electricity, water, minor repairs. Conservative buffer included each year.
